About the Artist

Where observation
meets imagination

Madeleine Town is a British artist based in the village of Saltford, nestled between Bath and Bristol. Her practice spans oil painting, printmaking, and richly layered mixed media — always drawing from the natural world.

Her paintings carry a rare emotional warmth: loose, expressive brushwork that still captures the essential character of her subjects. Whether a brooding Persian cat or a hyena crossing open grassland, each piece holds a moment of genuine connection.

Madeleine has worked across a range of scales and media for over two decades, producing originals, limited edition prints, and bespoke commissions for private collectors.

March 2025

Painting in the dark: night skies and white birds

There's something about working on a dark ground that changes everything. I've been experimenting with painting onto near-black canvases — building up light from nothing, letting the bird emerge from the night rather than sitting against it. It feels more true to how we actually see things at dusk.

January 2025

The hyena: misunderstood and magnificent

I've been drawn to animals that people don't usually find beautiful. The hyena's strange proportions make it the perfect subject — there's real dignity in those dark legs and amber coat once you look properly.

October 2024

Saltford: painting at the edge of the city

Living between Bath and Bristol gives me the best of both worlds — the village is quiet enough to think, but I'm never far from galleries, life drawing groups, and the visual stimulus of two brilliant cities.
